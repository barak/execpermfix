<H1>NAME</H1>
<P>
execpermfix - fixes executable permissions
</P>
<H1>SYNOPSIS</H1>
<P>
**execpermfix** [-h]
</P>
<P>
**execpermfix** [-V]
</P>
<P>
**execpermfix** [-v] [-n] &lt;files&gt;
</P>
<H1>DESCRIPTION</H1>
<P>
**execpermfix** is a program that fixes executable permissions.
</P>
<P>
It detects which files are executable by checking their names and contents, and
gives the executable permission to everyone that has read permissions.
</P>
<H1>OPTIONS</H1>
<P>
**-h** Help.
</P>
<P>
**-V** Version information.
</P>
<P>
**-v** Verbose mode.
</P>
<P>
**-n** Dry-run: no operation is performed. Use with <I>-v</I> to see files with
"wrong" permissions.
</P>
